Sponsored Research Projects

AI-on-Edge: AI-based Edge Analytics Framework for Real-time Disaster Management
Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt. of India (2021–2025)
Role: Principal Investigator
Problem Statement: Disaster-affected areas are often inaccessible, making real-time monitoring extremely challenging. This project aims to develop an edge-computing-based AI framework using UAV imagery to detect survivors and assess damage in real time, enabling faster and more effective disaster response.
Capacity Building for Human Resource Development in Unmanned Aircraft Systems
Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt. of India (2022–2027)
Role: Co-Principal Investigator
Problem Statement: The project focuses on developing national capacity in Unmanned Aircraft Systems by strengthening education, training, research, and innovation through collaborative academic programs, faculty development, and student skill-building initiatives.

Research Projects

Advanced Question Answering over Knowledge Graphs and Text Corpora
Postdoctoral Research, Max Planck Institute for Informatics, Germany (2019–2020).
Problem Statement: The goal was to answer complex multi-entity questions by dynamically joining evidence from unstructured text and curated knowledge graphs, overcoming incompleteness and staleness in existing KGs.
Analyzing the Structure and Dynamics of Multilayer Networks
Ph.D. Thesis, IIT Kharagpur (2013–2018).
Problem Statement: This research addressed community detection, information diffusion, node dynamics, and recommender systems in multilayer networks, bridging structural and functional network analysis.
