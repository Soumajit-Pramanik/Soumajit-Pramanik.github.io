About

Soumajit Pramanik is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Bhilai. He was previously a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, Germany, working with Prof. Gerhard Weikum. He received his Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur. His research focuses on machine learning and networked data, with particular emphasis on graph neural networks, natural language processing, question answering, computer vision, and complex multilayer networks.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning (Deep Learning, Graph Neural Networks)
  • Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval
  • Question Answering and Knowledge Graphs
  • Computer Vision and Edge AI
  • Complex and Multilayer Networks
